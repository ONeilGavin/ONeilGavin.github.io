PROHIBIT POLICE OFFICERS FROM HAVING SEX WITH DETAINEES

There is currently no law in Massachusetts that prohibits police officers from having sex with a detainee. It is not possible to give consent when someone is under arrest as the officers can user their power to force them into it. It allows for a loophole in which officers can get away with rape by claiming the detainee had given consent (Click here for a story that covers a time this happened).

A bill was written to prohibit this but it was never enacted. Contact your local representatives to demand that a bill be passed to prohibit police officers from having sex with detainees.
